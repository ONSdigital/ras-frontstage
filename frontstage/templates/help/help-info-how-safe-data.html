How do you keep my data safe?

We uphold our legal obligation to protect data in the following ways:

  • through the staff we employ
  • their training
  • the procedures we adopt
  • the IT equipment we use

We treat your data in strict confidence in line with the provisions of:

  • section 39 of the Statistics and Registration Services Act 2007
  • data protection legislation (including the General Data Protection Regulation and the Data Protection Act 2018)
  • the Code of Practice for Statistics

We only share your data with authorised ONS staff who need to be aware of them to compile statistics.

Any unauthorised disclosure of data would be a disciplinary and, in some cases criminal, offence.
