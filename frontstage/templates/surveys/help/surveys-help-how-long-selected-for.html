How long will my business be selected for?

We cannot give a definite period of selection for business surveys as this depends on various factors. We choose a specific number of businesses for a survey sample. This can change as businesses start and other businesses stop trading.

Employment levels within each business may change, which affects the number of businesses in each size-band. This also has an impact on how long a business is selected for.
