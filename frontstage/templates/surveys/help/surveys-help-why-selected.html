Why was my business selected?

We select businesses from the Inter-Departmental Business Register (IDBR). The IDBR is a register of businesses in the UK registered for PAYE, VAT or with Companies House.

Selection depends on several factors. For example, the number of people employed, how many other businesses are operating in the same industry and the size of those businesses.

We always include large businesses in the survey sample because the information they provide can be significant.

Large businesses are usually defined as having over 100 employees.
